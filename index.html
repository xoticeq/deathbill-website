The Law

Server Rules

01

No Cheating or Hacking

Cheaters are permanently banned and are not revived on wipe day. There are no exceptions.

02

Death is Final

When you die, you are banned until you purchase a revival or the monthly wipe occurs.

03

Respect Other Players

Racism, harassment, and hate speech will result in a permanent ban. PvP is part of the game. Toxicity is not.

04

No Exploiting Bugs

If you find a bug, report it. Exploiting glitches for unfair advantage is a bannable offense.

05

No Spawn Camping

Attacking players with active spawn protection is not allowed. Wait for it to expire.
